About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment institute for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles in regulated environments. We bridge the gap between academic insight through our UK university partnerships and real-world project deployment.

Academy

Intensive bootcamps and training for students and professionals entering the risk field.

Innovation

Developing IP, future risk models, and robust AI governance frameworks.

Advisory

Helping organisations implement critical frameworks like NIST, ISO 27001, and NIS2.

Our Community

We serve students, early-career professionals, and practitioners in financial services and critical infrastructure seeking to upskill in digital and cyber risk.
